We are a part of the SMASH program on the UC Berkeley campus. It is a summer academy where we live, study, and sleep 5 weeks in dorms on campus, while attending classes. In my Design Thinking class we are required to come up with a way to prevent a certain disease of our choice, and we chose STDs. The question we came up with to solve with our research is: How can we prevent STDs in teen living in the Bay Area? We hope to create a way to prevent STDs in teen living in the Bay Area.

QUESTIONS WE USED FOR INTERVIEWS:

1. How long have you been studying STDs?
2. What is a way to prevent/stop this disease from affecting any more people?
3. Do you know any specific information/statistics about STDs in teen living in the Bay Area?
4. Do you have any personal experience with these type of diseases?
5. When did you first hear about the effects of this disease?
